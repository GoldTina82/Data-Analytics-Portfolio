# 📊 Treasury Cash Flow Forecast & Dashboard (Excel)

## 🔹 Project Overview

This project is an Excel-based **Treasury Cash Flow Forecast and Dashboard** designed to monitor daily cash inflows and outflows, evaluate liquidity, and identify potential financial risks such as **low liquidity periods** and **cash gaps**.

The model simulates a real-world treasury scenario and demonstrates how financial data can be structured, analyzed, and visualized to support decision-making.

---

## 🔹 Key Features

* 📅 **30-day cash flow forecast**
* 💰 Tracking of **Cash In (receivables)** and **Cash Out (expenses, suppliers, payroll, etc.)**
* 📉 Calculation of:

  * Opening Balance
  * Net Cash Flow
  * Closing Balance
* ⚠️ **Liquidity risk classification**:

  * OK
  * Low Liquidity
  * Cash Gap
* 📊 **Pivot Table analysis**:

  * Cash flow by category
  * Liquidity risk distribution
* 📈 **Dashboard with charts and KPIs**
* 🔄 Use of **dynamic Excel Tables (structured references)**

---

## 🔹 Excel Concepts Used

* Structured References (Excel Tables)
* SUM / SUMIFS / COUNTIF
* INDEX & ROWS (dynamic last value)
* Conditional Formatting (risk highlighting)
* Pivot Tables & Charts
* Data Visualization (Line & Bar Charts)

---

## 🔹 Business Value

This project demonstrates how a company can:

* Monitor its **liquidity position in real time**
* Identify **timing mismatches between inflows and outflows**
* Detect potential **cash gaps before they occur**
* Support **financial planning and decision-making**

---

## 🔹 Example Insights

* Identification of days with **negative balance (cash gap)**
* Periods of **low liquidity before major inflows**
* Breakdown of expenses by category (e.g., suppliers, payroll)
